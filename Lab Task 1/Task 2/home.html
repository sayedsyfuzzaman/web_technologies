Here everyone can register himself/herself as a valuable donor by the Registration section of this site. Then Everyone throughout the country can see the their information as a volunteer blood donor and can contact with them in need of blood by the Search donor section.

Every registered donor can see and control his/her profile by login to his/her account from the Login section and update his/her information anytime. We strongly reccommend to every donor to update their 'blood donation record' regularly, when they'll donate blood. So that after the last donation date, no-one will see their information within four months.

Every registered donor can make a blood request to the Request blood section and anyone can make a response to the request. In the Blog section , anyone can write blogs related to blood, blood donation or any health issue. It will create social awareness.
